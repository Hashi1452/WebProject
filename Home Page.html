Centuries later, we are proud to share a legacy of true Sri Lankan hospitality with the world, in the finest of modern ecological luxury. And with a mystifying blend of rustic tradition and natural magnificence, we promise you a sojourn to remember. Welcome to Vil Uyana Resort.
